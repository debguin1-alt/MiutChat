CAPABILITIES

Built for the privacy-first world.

Every feature designed with one goal: leave no trace, hold no data, keep no secrets — not even from us.

End-to-End Encryption
AES-256-GCM encryption with PBKDF2 key derivation. Your room code is the key — only people who know it can read messages. The server only ever sees ciphertext.
Zero Accounts
No email. No phone. No password. No profile. Join any room instantly with just a code. Your anonymity is structural — there is nothing to link back to you.
Ephemeral Rooms
Rooms self-destruct when the last member leaves. No lingering history. Messages can also auto-vanish after 30 seconds, 5 minutes, or up to 7 days.
Real-Time Messaging
Instant delivery via Firestore. Typing indicators, read receipts, emoji reactions, replies, and encrypted file sharing — all anonymous, all real-time.
Offline Support
Messages cached locally with IndexedDB. The service worker ensures the app loads offline. Your history stays encrypted on your device only — never a cloud.
Signed Messages
Every message is signed with ECDSA P-256. Tampered or injected messages are automatically blocked. Key rotation every 200 messages keeps forward secrecy intact.
PRIVACY FIRST

We designed for zero knowledge.

Traditional chat apps know who you are, what you said, and when. MiutChat is architected to know nothing — by design, not just policy.

No data collectionWe don't collect names, emails, IPs, or message contents. Firebase Anonymous Auth gives you a disposable session ID only.
Client-side encryption onlyEncryption and decryption happen entirely in your browser via the Web Crypto API. The server only ever sees ciphertext it cannot read.
Auto-wipe on emptyWhen the last person leaves, all messages, members, and room data are permanently deleted. No admin can recover them.

Ready to disappear?

No account. No setup. Just create a room, share the code, and chat — knowing nothing you say can be traced back to you.
