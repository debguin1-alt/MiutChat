2. Service Description

MiutChat is a browser-based, ephemeral, anonymous group chat application employing client-side end-to-end encryption (AES-256-GCM), ECDSA message signing, and Firebase Firestore as a transient data relay. The Service is designed to facilitate private communication without persistent identity records.

The Service operates on a zero-knowledge architecture: messages are encrypted before reaching our infrastructure. The Operator cannot read, decode, or reconstruct any message content. All room data is permanently deleted when the last participant departs.
